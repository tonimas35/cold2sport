Cold2Sport nominated for Sports Technology Company of the Year at the 2025 European Technology Awards

At Cold2Sport, we are very proud to share some news that fills us with excitement and motivation: we have been nominated for Technology Company of the Year in the sports sector at the European Technology Awards 2025.

This recognition represents a great step forward for our team and validates the effort, passion, and innovation we put into everything we do every day. Since our inception, our goal has been to transform the way people enjoy, train, and improve in the world of sports through technology.

The European Technology Awards were created with the main purpose of enabling the attendees to share a day with other technology professionals, promoting excellence and knowledge through teaching, research, and proactive networking. 

The award-winners cover the most varied fields, working daily with great dedication in a sector in permanent evolution, where the update has immediate effects.

Professional and Business Excellence Institute is the organizer of these awards, which produces and publishes special reports in the mainstream media. 

We would like to deeply thank all our fans, customers, and partners for their constant support. Without you, this achievement would not be possible. Thank you for trusting us and for joining us on this path of growth and evolution.

This recognition motivates us to continue innovating and developing solutions that facilitate training, monitoring, and sports performance. At Cold2Sport, we believe that technology can be a fundamental ally for athletes of all levels to achieve their goals and enjoy their passion even more.

Let's keep growing together! We're excited about what's next and committed to continuing to lead innovation in the sports industry. See you at the next European Technology Awards on November 25th!
